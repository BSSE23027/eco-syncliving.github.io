Meet the Founder

EcoSync Living was founded by Rida Raheem Awan, an architect with a passion for sustainable and eco-friendly design. With years of experience in green architecture and a deep commitment to environmental preservation, Rida Raheem founded EcoSync Living to create living spaces that harmonize with nature while offering modern, functional design.

Rida's vision is to change the way we live by creating homes and spaces that not only serve as shelters but also contribute positively to the environment and future generations.
